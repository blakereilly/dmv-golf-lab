The Trackman iO is mounted overhead — out of your way — and captures every shot with radar-synchronized high-speed video. No floor pads, no interference, no guesswork.

Every session generates a precise data record: ball speed, club speed, spin rate, launch angle, carry distance, and impact video. This is how tour players practice. Now it's yours.

  • Full swing, short game, and putting data
  • Shot dispersion mapping across every club
  • Session history saved to your Trackman profile

From the Founder

I’m a normal guy obsessed with golf who happens to have a pretty technical day job. I studied Aerospace Engineering at Virginia Tech and by day, I work as a Satellite Operations Engineer in Northern Virginia. My team is responsible for flying 40 geostationary satellites that provide 24/7 connectivity around the globe.

I decided to start Capital Golf Labs because I got frustrated with the golf scene in the high-density parts of the DMV. You’re either stuck fighting traffic to get to a crowded outdoor range, paying for an exclusive country club, or trying to practice in a loud "golf-tainment" bar with a beer in your hand.

Golf is expensive, time-consuming, and a constant battle for consistency. I wanted to build a place that I would actually use—a space centered on three things:

Premium Tech: Access to the same data the pros use.

Focus: A private, comfortable environment without the crowds.

True Access: 24/7 availability for busy professionals.

I’m using my expertise in managing complex, 24/7 remote systems to create a studio that runs itself. You can book a session from your phone in 30 seconds, park right outside, and walk into a private studio with the Trackman iO warmed up and waiting for you.

Golf is hard enough. I want to make the practice part easy, fun, and available whenever you have an hour to spare.

Blake Founder, Capital Golf Labs
