Tour-Level Technology.
Available to You.

The Trackman iO is mounted overhead and captures every shot with radar-synchronized high-speed video. No floor pads, no interference, no guesswork.

Every session generates a precise data record: ball speed, club speed, spin rate, launch angle, carry distance, and impact video. This is how tour players practice. Now it's yours.

  • Overhead radar + radar-synchronized impact camera
  • Full swing, short game, and putting data
  • Shot dispersion mapping across every club
  • Session history saved to your Trackman profile

From the Founder

I'm a Virginia Tech Aerospace Engineering grad and Satellite Operations Engineer — I manage complex 24/7 remote systems for a living, and I'm obsessed with golf. I started Capital Golf Labs because the DMV doesn't have enough great places to practice, and nothing quite like this exists.

Every golfer would love a state-of-the-art sim in their basement or garage. Most of us don't have that luxury — and even fewer have access to a Trackman iO. So I'm building a private studio you can access like it's your own. Book from your phone, walk in, and start hitting with the same Trackman data tour players rely on. No staff. No waiting. No distractions.

Golf is hard enough. The practice part shouldn't be.

Blake Reilly Founder, Capital Golf Labs
