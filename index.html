The Concept

Capital Golf Labs is being designed as a quiet, private environment for golfers who want focused, data-driven practice.

Trackman-Powered

Planned Trackman launch monitors and high-speed camera setups for detailed swing and ball-flight analysis.

Practice-First Environment

No bar. No parties. No distractions. Just a space built around improvement.

Access Model (Planned)

We’re evaluating private sessions and membership access based on feedback from early interest.

What Capital Golf Labs Is — and Isn’t

What We Are

  • Trackman-powered practice studios
  • Private, quiet, focused environment
  • Appointment-based access (planned)
  • Built for golfers who want to improve

What We Aren’t

  • A bar or social venue
  • A loud group simulator space
  • Entertainment-first
  • Upsell-driven or time-pressured
